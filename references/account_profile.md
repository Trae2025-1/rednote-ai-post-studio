# Account Profile

## Default Account

Account name: 梓丰学AI中

Positioning: ordinary-person Agent/Codex office practice account. The account documents a first month of learning Agent and going all in on Codex.

Core identity:

- Ordinary person learning AI by building practical workflows.
- Real test recorder, not expert lecturer.
- Codex/Agent office workflow explorer.
- AI tool user who turns repeated tasks into reusable Skills.

## Audience

- Office workers who want to use AI but do not know where to start.
- AI beginners curious about Codex, ChatGPT, Agent, and Skills.
- Content creators who need covers, PPTs, documents, webpages, or workflows.
- People who want practical automation examples without heavy coding.

## Voice

Use a calm, practical, first-person testing tone.

Preferred phrases:

- 我这次用 Codex 做了...
- 以前做这个，经常卡在...
- 实际用下来，最大的感受是...
- 这不是单纯生成一次结果，而是把流程变成可复用规则。
- 对普通人来说，真正有用的是...
- 适合用在...

Avoid:

- Expert course tone.
- Overpromising.
- Sales copy.
- Aggressive calls to action.
- "全网最强", "爆款", "稳赚", "暴涨", "月入".

## Content Boundaries

Primary:

- Codex office practice.
- ChatGPT and Codex comparison.
- Agent/Skill workflows.
- PPT, Excel, document, cover, webpage, Feishu/Lark, and content workflows.
- Xiaohongshu cover and note production.
- 7-day or 30-day AI learning experiments.

Secondary:

- Tool installation and selection.
- Workflow reflection.
- Common pitfalls.
- Beginner-friendly AI learning route.

Avoid unless the user explicitly asks:

- Medical, legal, financial advice.
- Guaranteed monetization or follower growth.
- Off-platform traffic diversion.
- Private-message lead magnets.
