# Keyword Taxonomy

## Keyword Strategy

Use a mix of:

- Tool terms.
- Scenario terms.
- Ability terms.
- Audience terms.
- Platform terms.
- Long-tail search terms when relevant.

Do not claim a keyword is trending unless current web/platform research was performed in the same turn.
